🔒 Privacy & Security Information

BitVelo uses Android VPNService to route traffic locally on your device for real-time monitoring and filtering. No data is sent to external servers — everything happens on your device. Only one app can use VPNService at a time (Android limitation), so BitVelo cannot run simultaneously with other VPN apps.
